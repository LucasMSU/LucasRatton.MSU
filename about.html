About BetterBlinds

alt= "Shapes icon."

The Better Blinds product will come in two differnt housings. One will be CNC aluminum and the other will be plastic. The aluminum product housing will come out a premium price point. In the box you will recieve the case, cables and solar panel to charge the product

alt = "Internet Connection Logo"

During the intial setup you will be able to connect the product to your home wifi. This will allow you to control it in your smart home via the Better Blinds App!

alt = "App icon"

The Better Blinds App will be available on Android and Ios for download. This will be a free application. Within the app you can set a schedule to close once you leave your home.
